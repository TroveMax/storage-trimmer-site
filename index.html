How it works

Three steps to a cleaner library

Storage Trimmer does the hard work so you don't have to scroll through thousands of near-identical shots.

1

Scan your library

Choose a date range or scan your entire library. Storage Trimmer analyses every photo and video using Apple's on-device ML — no uploads, ever.

2

Review the clusters

Similar photos are grouped together. The sharpest, highest-quality version is automatically suggested as the keeper, and you can override at any time.

3

Delete and free up space

Select the duplicates you want to remove and delete them in one tap. Photos keeps deleted items in Recently Deleted for 30 days, just in case.

Features

Smarter than a simple file compare

Storage Trimmer understands your photos, not just their file sizes.

🔬

Visual similarity detection

Uses Apple Vision's neural feature prints to find photos that look alike — even if they're different exposures, crops, or edits of the same moment.

⭐️

Smart keeper selection

Automatically suggests the best photo to keep based on sharpness, resolution, whether you've favourited or edited it, and burst-pick selection.

🎬

Video support

Analyses video clips too, sampling multiple frames to find near-duplicate recordings that are eating your storage.

📅

Flexible date ranges

Scan just the last 30 days, a custom date range, or your entire library. Large libraries are processed in chunks to stay fast and responsive.

⚡️

Fast, non-blocking

Processing runs entirely in the background. Your Mac stays responsive while Storage Trimmer works through even the largest libraries.

🔒

No Photos left behind

Favourites are always protected. The app respects your existing curation — it works with your preferences, not against them.

Privacy

No analytics or tracking
